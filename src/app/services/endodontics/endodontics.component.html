Common Myths About Root Canals

Separating fact from fiction about endodontic treatment

Myth: Root canals are painful

Fact: Modern root canal therapy is no more painful than getting a filling. We use advanced anesthesia and techniques for your comfort.

Myth: Root canals cause illness

Fact: There is no scientific evidence linking root canals to illness. This myth has been thoroughly debunked by research.

Myth: Extraction is better than root canal

Fact: Saving your natural tooth with a root canal is almost always better than extraction. Natural teeth maintain jaw health and function.

Myth: Root canals require multiple visits

Fact: Most root canals can be completed in one or two visits, depending on the complexity of your case.
