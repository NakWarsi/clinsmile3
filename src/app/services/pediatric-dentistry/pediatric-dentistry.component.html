Preparing for Your Child's First Visit

Make the first dental visit a positive experience

Before the Visit

  • Read books about dental visits
  • Role-play with a toothbrush
  • Explain what to expect
  • Choose a comfortable time

During the Visit

  • Stay calm and positive
  • Let the dentist lead
  • Provide comfort if needed
  • Ask questions

After the Visit

  • Praise good behavior
  • Discuss the experience
  • Schedule follow-up
  • Maintain good habits
